Hey, This is my first complete end to end deployment project. I have used the Wisconsin Breast Cancer Data. The model being used under the hood is a CatBoostClassifier. I tried 5 different ensemble models like, GradientBoosting, XGBoost, RandomForest, LightGBM & CatBoost. Out of all CatBoost performed the best with an accuracy of about 92%.

So, I chose to train another CatBoostClassifier, so as to pass it into a Soft Voting Ensemble Classifier with a purpose of increasing the accuracy. But, the second indivitual trained CatBoostClassifier outperformed both the first CatBoostClassifier as well as the SoftVotingClassifier (two CatBoostClassifiers). The accuracy of second CatBoostClassifier was 96% on Validation set, & 97% on Test set, where as the accuracy of SoftVotingClassifier was 95% on Validation Set. Hence, I chose the second CatBoostClassifier.
